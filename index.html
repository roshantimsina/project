Vehicle Information System(E-SAFARI)

Vehicle Information System is proposed with the intention of upgrading the current stand-alone system to web based or online system. Hopefully with this idea, this system is capable to help smooth and enhance vehicle management of Nepal. Basically , the proposed system of vehicle Information is all about process of abstracting information about the vehicle of associate route and its detailed information regarding the shortest possible route among various options, vehicle standard fare authorized by Ministry of Transportation of Nepal Government, vehicle reservation, calling cab through SMS gateway etc.


The system is all about helping the common people getting access through actual information regarding the accessibility of vehicles which sole objective is to implement standarization on vechiles fare rate of both public and private vehicles and prevents people from fraud and getting cheated.This web app is likely to feature data provided by Traffic Control Police and also a map view where people can enter the location of their source and destination.
